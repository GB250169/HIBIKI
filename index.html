MBE10503: Educational Technology in TVET

Interactive Electronics

Lesson Plan

A digital-focused curriculum integrating Autodesk Tinkercad simulations to bridge the gap in traditional electronics education.

About the Project

This teaching material is designed to revolutionize how TVET students approach electronics. By shifting from static diagrams to dynamic 3D simulations, we provide a safe, scalable, and engaging environment.

Core Objectives

  • ✓ Bridge theoretical knowledge with practical simulation skills.
  • ✓ Reduce barriers to entry for high-cost components.

1.0 Introduction

1.1 Background of the Study

Electronics education is a cornerstone of modern Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET). Traditionally, students learn through physical breadboarding and theoretical lectures. This study focuses on how computer simulations, specifically Autodesk Tinkercad, can enhance conceptual understanding.

1.2 Problem Statement

  • ! Resource Scarcity: High cost of physical components limits student access.
